# User Research

> House rules, connectors, and the design bible: see [CONNECTORS.md](../../CONNECTORS.md).

Help plan, execute, and synthesize user research for Alongside-family products.

## Who the users are (start here)

Don't research in the abstract — these are the real audiences:

- **DIYers & small/solo shops** — Foxhole Academy and the diagnostic-consulting
  offer (per-incident calls, retainer/lifeline, workflow teardowns).
- **Shop owners/techs** — THE APP (shop management software). Rural shops lose
  connectivity regularly; offline behavior is a genuine differentiator worth
  testing directly.
- **Spanish-speaking customers & mixed-status communities** — Translation SaaS.
  **Handle with extra care** (see ethics below).
- **Content audience** — OrcaTeknic / Foxhole viewers, coaching funnel.

## Research Methods

| Method | Best For | Sample | Time |
|--------|----------|--------|------|
| User interviews | Needs & motivations | 5–8 | 2–4 wk |
| Usability testing | Evaluating a flow/artifact | 5–8 | 1–2 wk |
| Surveys | Quantifying attitudes | 100+ | 1–2 wk |
| Card sorting | Information architecture | 15–30 | 1 wk |
| Diary studies | Behavior over time | 10–15 | 2–8 wk |
| A/B testing | Specific design choices | Stat. significance | 1–4 wk |

## Interview Guide Structure

1. **Warm-up** (5 min) — rapport, explain the session
2. **Context** (10 min) — their current workflow (e.g. how they diagnose now,
   how they handle a Spanish-speaking customer today)
3. **Deep dive** (20 min) — the specific topic
4. **Reaction** (10 min) — show the artifact/prototype
5. **Wrap-up** (5 min) — anything missed? thank them.

## Ethics & data minimization (non-negotiable)

This mirrors the Translation SaaS posture and Kayce's standing values — apply it
to *all* research, not just that product:

- **Collect the minimum.** Don't capture immigration status, legal status, or
  other sensitive fields. For mixed-status communities this isn't a preference,
  it's safety infrastructure.
- **Consent is plain and real.** Explain what's recorded, why, how long it's kept,
  and that they can stop anytime.
- **Local/owned by default.** Keep notes and recordings in owned storage, not a
  third-party analytics platform. Auto-purge raw data on a stated schedule.
- **No dark-pattern recruiting.** No manipulative incentives or pressure.
- **Honest synthesis.** Separate observation from interpretation; don't overclaim
  from a small sample.

## Analysis Framework

- **Affinity mapping** — group observations into themes
- **Impact/effort matrix** — prioritize findings
- **Journey mapping** — visualize the experience over time
- **Jobs to be done** — what they're hiring the product to do

## Deliverables

- Research plan (objectives, methods, timeline, participants, **consent + data
  handling plan**)
- Interview guide (questions, probes, activities)
- Synthesis report (themes, insights, recommendations) — see /research-synthesis
- Highlight reel (key quotes, anonymized)
