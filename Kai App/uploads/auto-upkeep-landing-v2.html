The Reality

The Shop
Doesn't Work
For You

"A diagnostic is $150 in-shop. $250 if they come to you. Most people don't know what they're paying for."

— Real numbers, DFW area, 2025

The automotive industry runs on information asymmetry. You don't know what you don't know — and shops count on that. The result: services you didn't need, bills you can't argue, and a feeling that every shop visit is a gamble.

  • You get told you need service X with no way to verify it
  • Maintenance schedules are designed to maximize shop visits, not your car's lifespan
  • Warning lights are engineered to create anxiety, not inform you
  • YouTube exists — but nobody to ask your specific question
  • The people who know the most charge the most to access them

FAQ

Common Questions

I know nothing about cars. Is this for me? +
Yes — that's exactly who this is for. You don't need any background knowledge. We start where you are. Nobody gets made to feel dumb for asking a basic question. The community is intentionally welcoming to all experience levels.
What is Discord and do I need to know how to use it? +
Discord is a free app — think group chat meets forum. It works on your phone, tablet, or computer. If you can send a text message, you can use Discord. A short welcome guide walks you through everything when you join.
Does this cover my specific car or motorcycle? +
The fundamentals — fluids, tires, brakes, warning lights, maintenance schedules — apply to nearly every vehicle. For make/model specific questions, that's what the community channels and 1:1 sessions are for. You're not getting generic YouTube content.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Discord memberships cancel anytime with no runaround, no phone call required. You keep access through the end of your billing period.
Why doesn't Under the Hood include 1:1 sessions? +
Under the Hood is built around the group coaching experience — two sessions a month, live shop demos, community access. It doesn't include a 1:1 session in the tier price, but you can add one on at $125. If you want a 1:1 included every month without thinking about it, Full Service is the right move.
How do I pay? Do I have to pay inside the Discord app? +
No — and you shouldn't. All memberships are purchased through a browser link, not inside the Discord app. This keeps pricing consistent regardless of whether you're on iPhone, Android, or desktop. Your Discord role is assigned automatically after payment.
Is this community welcoming to LGBTQ+ folks and women? +
Yes. Full stop. The automotive space has a long history of being hostile to people who aren't straight men, and this community is built to be different. Everyone here is working on the same goal: understanding their vehicle. The knowledge doesn't care who you are.
